Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

On Sri Lanka's southern coast, the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery represents hope for the survival of sea turtles, which are threatened with extinction. This remarkable hatchery has dedicated itself to protecting turtle populations and raising awareness of their importance ever since it was formed in 1981. Kosgoda has made significant contributions to turtle conservation through innovation, helping to protect nesting sites, cure injured turtles, and release hatchlings into the ocean. The hatchery offers visitors the chance to see these extinct species up close, learn about their life cycles, and participate in their preservation. An outstanding illustration of the constant commitment and devotion required to ensure the survival of these sea turtles.
